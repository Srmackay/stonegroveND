Graduate outcomes
Employment rates, salary bands, and the autistic employment paradox
Autistic graduates: 69.5% employed — highest of any group including non-NDDespite lower degree classifications (6% Firsts vs 10.6%), autistic graduates outperform on employment and earn comparably to non-ND.
